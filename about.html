About

I wrote my first line of code when I was five years old. It was in BASIC, using my family's old Amstrad CPC 464. Though I couldn't sweat to it, I'm guessing it was something along the lines of:

10 PRINT "NICK IS GREAT"
20 GOTO 10

As I got little older, and my family bought slightly more modern hardware, I started experimenting with making web pages: fan-sites for video games I liked, or attempts to write Choose Your Own Adventure-style games in pure HTML. Horrifyingly, some of this juvenilia is accessible through archive.org. I'll never tell where.

I don't work with BASIC anymore, but I do work with HTML, alongside CSS and Javascript. I also have some experience with SQL, C, Ruby and Dart.
